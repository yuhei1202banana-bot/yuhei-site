What changed me

I used to travel alone, looking for new experiences. One day in Turkey, I was on a hot air balloon. The view was beautiful. But what stayed with me was not the scenery. It was the woman next to me. She was looking at the same view — and she was crying. I didn’t understand why. And strangely, I wasn’t enjoying it as much as I thought I would. After that, I tried many things. But something felt missing. Then I noticed something. The guide looked genuinely happy. That was the moment I realized — I don’t just want to experience something. I want to create something for someone else. That is why I started building websites. I want to help express what makes a place special, so that someone else can feel something real.

How I see the world

I care about atmosphere, memory, and emotion.
